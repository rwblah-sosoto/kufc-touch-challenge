Privacy Policy

KUFC Touch Challenge App  ·  Last updated: May 2026

This Privacy Policy describes how CompuRay Technologies ("we", "us", or "our") collects, uses, and protects information in connection with the KUFC Touch Challenge mobile application ("the App"), operated on behalf of Kamloops United FC ("KUFC").

1. Who This App Is For

The App is used by youth soccer players and their parents/guardians participating in the KUFC Touch Challenge program. Player accounts are created by authorized coaches only. No one can self-register.

2. Information We Collect

We collect the following information when coaches register players and when players use the App:

  • Player name — used to identify the player on the leaderboard
  • Email address — used for account login and password reset
  • Age group / team — U11, U12, or other KUFC age groups
  • Touch submissions — which touch types were completed, how many touches, and which day of the challenge
  • Submission timestamps — when each submission was made

We do not collect location data, payment information, photos, or any other personal information beyond what is listed above.

3. How We Use Your Information

  • To allow players to log their daily training touches
  • To display progress on the team leaderboard
  • To allow coaches to monitor player participation and progress
  • To send password reset emails when requested

We do not use your information for advertising, marketing to third parties, or any purpose other than operating the KUFC Touch Challenge program.

4. Who Can See Your Information

  • Players can see their own touch submissions and progress
  • All team members can see the leaderboard (player names and percentage complete)
  • Coaches can see all player data for their team
  • CompuRay Technologies has administrative access for technical support purposes only

We do not sell, share, or disclose your information to any third parties except as described in Section 5.

5. Third-Party Services

The App uses the following third-party services to operate:

  • Google Firebase (Google LLC) — provides user authentication, database storage, and app hosting. Data is stored on Google's servers. See Google's Privacy Policy.

These services are bound by their own privacy policies and comply with applicable data protection laws.

6. Data Retention

Player data is retained for the duration of the KUFC Touch Challenge season. At the end of each season, coaches may request deletion of all player data. You may also request deletion of your account and data at any time by contacting us.

7. Children's Privacy

The App is used by youth players, some of whom may be under 13. We do not knowingly collect more information than necessary from any user. Player accounts are created exclusively by authorized KUFC coaches — children cannot self-register. Parents or guardians who wish to review or delete their child's data should contact their KUFC coach or reach us directly at the address below.

8. Data Security

We use industry-standard security measures including encrypted data transmission (HTTPS), Firebase Authentication for secure login, and Firestore security rules that restrict data access based on user role. Only coaches can create or delete player accounts.

9. Your Rights

You have the right to:

  • Request access to the personal data we hold about you
  • Request correction of inaccurate data
  • Request deletion of your account and associated data

To exercise any of these rights, contact your KUFC coach or reach us directly below.

10. Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. We will notify users of any significant changes through the App or by email. Continued use of the App after changes constitutes acceptance of the updated policy.
